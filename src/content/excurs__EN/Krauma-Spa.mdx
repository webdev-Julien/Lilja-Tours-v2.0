Located at Deildartunguhver, Europe’s most powerful hot spring, Krauma Geothermal Baths & Spa offers a pure and luxurious spa experience in the heart of Icelandic nature. Just a short drive from Reykholt in West Iceland, Krauma lets you feel the energy of the Earth through naturally heated geothermal water that reaches 100°C (212°F) — no additives or chemicals required.

---

## The Krauma Experience

Krauma features a thoughtfully designed spa that blends comfort with nature’s raw elements. Thanks to the rapid flow of Deildartunguhver, the pools are constantly refreshed, making the bathing experience exceptionally clean and natural.

### What’s Included:

- Five natural geothermal baths at varying temperatures
- One cold tub for an invigorating boost to your circulation
- Steam bath and infrared room
- Relaxation room with a cozy fireplace and calming music
- Private lockers in modern changing rooms

---

## Reconnect & Recharge

The setting is tranquil and scenic, with views of the steaming hot spring just outside. After soaking, take your time in the relaxation room, designed to bring body and mind into complete calm.

For the full experience, we recommend enjoying a meal at the Krauma Restaurant, where you'll find Icelandic dishes made with fresh, locally sourced farm ingredients. Whether you're dining indoors or outside on the terrace, the views and atmosphere are equally unforgettable. The bar offers a selection of drinks and small bites — perfect for winding down.

### On-Site Amenities:
