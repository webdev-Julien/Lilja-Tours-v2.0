Located in the heart of North Iceland’s volcanic landscape, the Mývatn Nature Baths offer a peaceful and rejuvenating geothermal spa experience. This traditional yet tastefully designed bathing complex invites you to relax in geothermal mineral-rich waters, surrounded by stunning natural scenery unique to the Mývatn region.

---

## What Awaits You

At Mývatn Nature Baths, you’ll find a soothing mix of natural elements and modern comfort. The facility includes:

- Two steam baths built directly above an active geothermal area where sulfur steam rises naturally through vents in the floor
- A geothermal lagoon with mineral-rich, blue water
- A large hot tub with panoramic views of the lava fields and surrounding hills

The water is sourced from a 2,500-meter-deep borehole, rich in minerals and naturally alkaline, making it ideal for bathing and gentle on the skin.

---

## Included with Your Ticket:

- Full access to the steam baths, geothermal lagoon, and hot tub
- Locker use in the changing rooms
- Clean and well-maintained changing and shower facilities

---

## Café Kvika

Before or after your soak, visit Café Kvika, located on-site and offering light meals, snacks, and drinks. Sit back and enjoy the breathtaking views of the surrounding volcanic terrain and Lake Mývatn area as you unwind.

---

### What to Bring:

- Swimsuit and towel (available to rent on-site if needed)
- Warm clothes for after bathing
- Waterproof bag if you plan to bring electronics
