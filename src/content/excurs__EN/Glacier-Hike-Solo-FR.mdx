Join us on a glacier hike like no other as we explore the stunning ice formations, crevasses, and natural sculptures of Sólheimajökull Glacier—one of Iceland’s most accessible and awe-inspiring outlet glaciers. This small group adventure combines expert-led glacier hiking with professional photography, giving you not only the experience of a lifetime but also the photos to prove it.

Whether you're new to glacier hiking or have done it before, this tour is designed for all levels of adventurers.

---

## Tour Highlights

- Certified glacier guide and photographer to lead and capture your journey
- Access to small group experience (max 10 guests per guide) for a personalized adventure
- Unique photo package with 2 professionally edited shots (portraits, action & landscape)
- Explore dramatic ice features like moulins, ridges, and vivid blue crevasses
- Learn about glacier formation and evolution from your guide along the way

---

### Meeting Point & Preparation

Your tour begins at the Sólheimajökull Glacier parking lot (871 Vík). Just search "Ice Pic Journeys" in Google Maps to find us. We recommend arriving 15 minutes early to check in and use the restrooms before the hike, as facilities are limited at the glacier.

Once checked in, you’ll board a super jeep for a short 5-minute ride to the glacier, where your guide will help you gear up with:

- Helmet, harness, crampons, and ice axe
- Hiking boots (available for rent if needed)

---

## Glacier Hike Details

- Total hike duration: ~2.5 hours
  - First half: Flat, rocky terrain (easy)
  - Second half: Gradual incline onto the glacier (moderate effort)
- Your guide will lead you onto the ice and explain glacier hiking techniques, safety tips, and the natural processes that shape this icy world.

## You’ll Explore:

- Vibrant blue ice formations
- Deep crevasses and narrow ice ridges
- Moulins (vertical shafts formed by meltwater)
- Ever-changing ice sculptures and patterns

Plenty of time is allocated for photos and exploration.
