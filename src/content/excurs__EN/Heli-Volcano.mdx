## Unleash Your Sense of Adventure from Above!

Take to the skies on an unforgettable helicopter tour over Iceland’s latest volcanic eruption sites! Departing from Reykjavik Domestic Airport—just a short 5-minute drive from the city center—you'll soar above some of the most awe-inspiring natural phenomena Iceland has to offer. This unique aerial journey promises unparalleled views of molten lava flowing from active volcanic craters.

### Discover the Latest Volcanic Eruptions in Reykjanes Peninsula:

- **Fagradalsfjall**: March 19th – September 2021  
- **Meradalir**: August 3rd, 2022  
- **Litli-Hrútur**: July 10th/August 2023  
- **Sundhnúkur**: December 2023  
- **New Eruptions**: January 14th, February 8th, and March 20th, 2024  

### What to Expect from Your Helicopter Tour

This round-trip flight lasts approximately **30-35 minutes** and covers both the oldest and newest eruption sites. Please note that due to safety regulations, landing at the eruption site is not possible, but you will experience the most breathtaking aerial perspectives of the lava fields and craters.

All flights are subject to weather conditions and regulations set by the **Department of Civil Protection and Emergency Management**. While volcanic activity is unpredictable, we ensure the best possible viewing experience for every tour.

### Important Information

After booking, we will confirm the **exact departure time**. Please provide your **phone number, email, and approximate weight for each passenger**. 

If weather conditions do not allow the tour at the scheduled time, we offer three alternatives:  
1. **Rescheduling on the Same Day:** If possible, we will adjust the time (e.g., from morning to afternoon).  
2. **Alternative Dates:** We may offer a flight the day before or after your scheduled date.  
3. **Full Refund:** If rescheduling is not possible, we guarantee a full refund.
