## An Epic Aerial Journey Through Iceland's Natural Wonders

Take off from Reykjavik on an exhilarating helicopter tour that showcases the natural beauty of Iceland from above. This tour takes you over majestic waterfalls, ancient volcanoes, and the iconic Þingvellir National Park. With one scenic landing included, you’ll experience Iceland's raw landscapes up close.

### Tour Highlights

- Glymur Waterfall: Discover Iceland's tallest waterfall, hidden deep within a narrow canyon at the base of Hvalfjörður (Whalefjord). The sheer drop and surrounding scenery are simply breathtaking.
- Skjaldbreiður Volcano: Fly over this ancient shield volcano, which last erupted 9,000 years ago. Its vast lava flow formed the foundation of Þingvellir Lake and the national park.
- Þingvellir National Park: Follow the Continental Rift through the park, witnessing dramatic crevices and geological formations caused by the diverging tectonic plates of Eurasia and North America.

### What’s Included

- Flight Duration: Approximately 80 minutes
- Landing: 15-minute stopover at a scenic location
- Capacity: Up to 4 passengers per helicopter

### Important Information

Flights are subject to weather conditions. In case of unsuitable weather, we strive to provide an alternative landing spot or reschedule your flight.
