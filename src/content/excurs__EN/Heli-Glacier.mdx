## Explore Iceland from the Skies with a Glacier Landing

Embark on an exhilarating helicopter journey that showcases Iceland's dramatic landscapes from above! Departing from Reykjavík Airport, this tour takes you on an unforgettable aerial adventure, featuring stunning waterfalls, glaciers, and volcanic terrain.

### Scenic Highlights of Your Tour

- Glymur Waterfall: One of Iceland’s tallest and most breathtaking waterfalls.
- Þórisjökull Glacier: Enjoy a 15-minute stopover (weather permitting), where you’ll be surrounded by panoramic mountain vistas.
  - _Alternative Landing:_ If conditions prevent landing at the glacier, we will select a scenic alternative stop.
- Skjaldbreiður Volcano: Witness the expansive slopes of this 9,000-year-old shield volcano.
- Þingvellir National Park: Fly over the iconic rift valley where the Eurasian and North American tectonic plates meet.

### What’s Included

- Flight Duration: Approximately 90 minutes
- Landing: 15-minute stopover (weather dependent)
- Capacity: Up to 4 passengers per helicopter

### Cancellation Policy

- 100% charge if canceled within 2 days of the event
- 50% charge if canceled within 7 days of the event
- 10% charge if canceled within 14 days of the event

### Important Information

All flights are subject to weather conditions. In case of adverse weather, we will always strive to offer a suitable alternative stop or reschedule.
