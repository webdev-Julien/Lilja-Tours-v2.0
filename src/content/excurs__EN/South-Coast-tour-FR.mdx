Departing from Reykjavík, this full-day small group tour takes you along the stunning South Coast of Iceland, a region where black sand beaches, cascading waterfalls, glaciers, and volcanoes all converge in breathtaking harmony. With a French-speaking expert guide and a maximum of 12 participants, this is a personal and immersive adventure through one of Iceland’s most cinematic and surreal landscapes.

From the volcanic cliffs and glacial peaks to locations used in films and series like _Star Wars_ and _Game of Thrones_, every stop will leave you in awe of Iceland’s raw, untamed beauty.

---

## Tour Overview

- Duration: Approx. 11–12 hours (pick-up and drop-off included)
- Group Size: Max 12 people
- Guide: Experienced French-speaking guide
- Departure Points:
  - Hallgrímskirkja, Reykjavík
  - Harpa Concert Hall, Reykjavík
- Frequency: One departure every two weeks
- Minimum Participants: 4 required to confirm departure

> _If the minimum is not reached, you can opt to reserve the remaining seats. If more guests register later, your additional payment will be refunded._

## What You'll See (Indicative Itinerary):

- Black sand beaches with towering basalt cliffs
- Waterfalls of all shapes and sizes, sometimes frozen in winter
- Volcanic mountains topped with glaciers
- Scenic vistas that have served as backdrops in major film and TV productions

> _Note: The final itinerary may be adjusted depending on weather and local conditions. The guide has the discretion to make any necessary changes to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience._

---

### What to Bring:

- Warm and waterproof clothing
- Walking shoes or hiking boots
- Sunglasses and sunscreen (yes—even in Iceland!)
