## Immerse Yourself in Tranquility

Discover a truly authentic Icelandic experience at the Húsafell Canyon Baths. Nestled in a remote and peaceful highland setting, these geothermal pools offer a unique opportunity to relax in the pristine wilderness. After hiking through the rugged landscapes of the highlands, nothing compares to soaking in the warm, natural waters of the canyon baths.

---

## A Sustainable and Authentic Retreat

The Húsafell locals who developed the Canyon Baths were committed to preserving the natural beauty of the area. The entire structure is environmentally unobtrusive, crafted with sustainability in mind, and respects the region’s rich heritage. The result is an immersive and respectful experience that blends seamlessly with the surrounding landscape.

## Why Visit Húsafell Canyon Baths?

- Solitude and Serenity: Enjoy a tranquil soak far from city life.
- Scenic Hiking Trails: Combine a rewarding hike with a relaxing geothermal dip.
- Local Expertise: Led by an English-speaking guide who shares insights into the area.
- Environmentally Conscious: Built to harmonize with the natural surroundings.

---

## Tour Details:

- Duration: Approximately 2 hours (including transport)
- Difficulty: Moderate (hiking involved)
- Meeting Point: Hotel Húsafell

### What's Included:

- Entry to Húsafell Canyon Baths
- Local, English-speaking guide
- Transport to and from Hotel Húsafell
- Towel for drying off
- Gratuities

### What to Bring:

- Warm, waterproof outdoor clothing
- Hiking boots
- Swimsuit (can be rented at Hotel Húsafell swimming pool if needed)
- Camera to capture the breathtaking scenery

---

### Important Notes:

- Alcohol Restriction: Guests are not permitted to consume their own alcohol in the baths.
- Comfortable Attire: Wear suitable hiking clothes and sturdy footwear.
- Safety First: Follow your guide’s instructions and stay on marked trails.

---

### Cancellation Policy:

- 100% cancellation fee if canceled 1 day or less before the event.
