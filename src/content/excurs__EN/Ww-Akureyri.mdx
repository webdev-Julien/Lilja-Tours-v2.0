## Explore Iceland’s Longest Fjord on a Whale Watching Adventure

Embark on an unforgettable journey through Eyjafjörður, Iceland’s longest fjord, and witness the majestic whales of the North Atlantic in their natural habitat. Our expert whale watching guides will lead you on a 2.5 to 3.5 hour tour, sharing their knowledge about local wildlife, nature, environmental insights, and the history of the Eyjafjörður region.

---

## What Makes This Tour Unique?

Our tours are operated on three large, custom-designed boats built specifically for whale watching in Iceland’s rugged coastal waters. The largest vessel in our fleet is Iceland’s only high-speed, specially modified whale watching ship, designed to comfortably carry up to 200 passengers while offering unbeatable viewing opportunities.

### Vessel Highlights:

- Cinema-style viewing platforms at the front of the ship
- Indoor and outdoor seating with ample space for everyone
- Onboard toilets, bar, and cafeteria
- High-speed capability ensures sustainable, efficient whale sightings

---

## Tour Information:

- Duration: 2.5 – 3.5 hours
- Departure location:
  - Elding Ticket Office, located by the floating pier near the HOF Cultural Centre, Akureyri
- Departure frequency: Several times a day during summer season
- Parking: Free parking available next to the office
- Accessibility: Walking distance from most accommodations in Akureyri

---

### What to Bring:

- Warm outdoor clothing (fleece or wool layers are ideal)
- Hat and gloves for extra comfort on deck
- Flat-soled shoes for safe footing
- Camera (optional – photos are also provided after the tour)

---

### What’s Included:

- Expertly guided whale watching tour in Eyjafjörður
- Use of warm overalls to stay comfortable
- Live commentary on wildlife, environmental issues, and local history
- Access to indoor and outdoor facilities on board
- Free photos taken by the crew during the tour
- “Whale guarantee” – if you don’t see a whale, you can join another tour for free within the next 2 years (in Akureyri or Reykjavík)

---

### Cancellation Policy:

- 100% cancellation fee if canceled 1 day or less before the tour
