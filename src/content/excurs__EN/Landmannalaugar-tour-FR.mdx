Set off from Reykjavík on a full-day adventure to Landmannalaugar, one of Iceland’s most breathtaking highland gems. Known for its multi-colored rhyolite mountains, vast lava fields, and geothermal activity, this area offers some of the country’s most unreal and surreal scenery. Accompanied by a French-speaking expert guide, you'll explore this magical region in an intimate group of no more than 12 participants.

Cap off your hike with a soak in a natural geothermal hot spring—the perfect reward after exploring this remote wonderland. Don’t forget your swimsuit!

---

## Tour Overview

- Duration: Approx. 11–12 hours (pick-up and drop-off included)
- Group Size: Max 12 people
- Language: French-speaking guide
- Departure Frequency: Once a week, from mid-June to mid-September (subject to road conditions)
- Departure Points:
  - Hallgrímskirkja & Harpa, Reykjavík
  - Selfoss
  - Hella
  - Hvolsvöllur
- Minimum Participants: 4 required to confirm departure

> _If the minimum number is not reached, you may opt to reserve additional spots to ensure the tour runs. If more participants book later, you’ll be refunded the difference._
