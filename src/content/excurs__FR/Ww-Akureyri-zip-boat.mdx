## Discover the Whales of Skjálfandi Bay with Gentle Giants

Embark on the Traditional Whale Watching Tour from the picturesque fishing town of Húsavík, often dubbed the Whale Watching Capital of Europe. This authentic sea adventure takes you into the rich waters of Skjálfandi Bay, framed by the majestic mountains of Vík, and offers you the chance to witness the gentle giants of the ocean in their natural habitat.

This tour is operated by a family-run local company with 160 years of heritage in the area. With deep roots in Skjálfandi Bay, their connection to the land and sea brings an unmatched authenticity to every journey.

---

## What Makes This Tour Special?

- Traditional oak boats for a timeless Icelandic seafaring experience
- Local guides who know the bay’s history, wildlife, and secrets
- Views of whales, dolphins, seabirds, and the untouched beauty of North Iceland
- A glimpse into the natural harmony that defined the lives of Icelandic ancestors

---

## Tour Details:

- Duration: Approximately 3 hours
- Departure Location: Gentle Giants Ticket Center, Húsavík Harbour
- Boat Type: Traditional oak whale watching boat
- Check-In Time: At least 40 minutes before departure
- Accessibility:
  - Wheelchair accessible
  - Suitable for guests with limited mobility or vision impairments
- Weather Policy: Schedule changes may occur due to weather or sea conditions for safety and comfort

---

### What’s Included:

- Departure tax & admission fees
- Expert whale watching guide on board
- Warm safety overalls (worn over your clothes)
- Rain jackets if needed
- Goods & services tax

---

### What to Bring:

- Warm and waterproof clothing
- Hats, gloves, and sturdy footwear
- Protective bags for cameras (it can get cold and wet at sea)
- While safety overalls are provided, they are worn over your existing layers, so dress accordingly

---

### Cancellation Policy:

- 100% cancellation fee if canceled 1 day or less before departure
